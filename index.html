CollegeSwap

An Android app to allow UA students to buy and sell textbooks, football tickets, and subleases.

What is College Swap?

College Swap is an Android app and server component designed to help college students at The University of Alabama buy and sell textbooks, football tickets, and subleases.

Project Goals

  • Provide an Android app to facilitate common trades between students
  • Create a server component to host the listings
  • Create an interface to effectively allow students to find listings for the exact item they want
  • Help college students save money

Why College Swap?

Currently, many students use Facebook for common trades. Facebook is convenient and accessible, but the interface is terrible for buying and selling items.

Unlike platforms like Craigslist and Facebook, our app offers fields specific to the types of goods college students trade. Instead of typing keywords and hoping to find relevant results, students can choose a category (like textbooks) and search by specific fields (like course number) to return precisely what they want to find.

What types of goods are supported?

Textbooks, football tickets, and subleases are the only supported categories. These were determined to be the items most commonly traded.
